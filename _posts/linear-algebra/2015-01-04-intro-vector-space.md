#### Vector space

#### Example 1:

##### Definition:
$$
a = (x_1, y_1) ;
b = (x_2, y_2) ;
$$

##### Plus:
$$
a + b  = (x_1 + x_1, y_1 + y_2) ;
$$

##### Mutiply:
$$
\alpha \cdot a = (\alpha \cdot x_1, \alpha \cdot y_1) ;
$$

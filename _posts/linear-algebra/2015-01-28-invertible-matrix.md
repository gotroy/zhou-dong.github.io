#### Definition:  
$$ AB = BA = I_n $$  

##### Identity Matrix ( Identity Matrix is an Square Matrix ):  
$$
 I_n = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & \cdots & 0 \\ \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ 0 &  \cdots & 1 \end{pmatrix}_{n \times n} \\
 I_mA_{mn} = A_{mn}I_n = A \\
$$
